Mission Brief

Why Most Notes
Are Useless

Most people are transcribers — they record everything and process nothing. A trained operative is a processor: capturing signal, discarding noise, connecting patterns invisible to the untrained eye.

This program runs you through 7 levels of increasing sophistication — from writing your first structured note to building memory palaces, reading rooms in 4 seconds, and thinking three moves ahead.
